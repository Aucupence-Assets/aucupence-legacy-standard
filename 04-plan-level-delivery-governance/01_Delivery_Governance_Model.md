## 1. Governance Layers

1. **Executive Steering Committee (Monthly / Quarterly)**
   - Composition: Customer Sponsor, Vendor Engagement Director, Finance.
   - Focus: Business outcomes, budget vs. actuals, major risks and decisions.

2. **Engagement Governance (Bi-weekly)**
   - Composition: Product Owners, Project Manager, Solution Architect.
   - Focus: Scope decisions, roadmap trade-offs, cross-team dependencies.

3. **Operational/Squad Level (Weekly / Daily)**
   - Composition: Scrum teams, Tech Leads, QA, DevOps.
   - Focus: Sprint goals, incident review, continuous improvement actions.

## 2. Standard Ceremonies & Artefacts

- **Steering Committee Pack**
  - KPI dashboard (delivery, quality, financial).
  - Key decisions log (with owners and due dates).
  - Top 5 risks and mitigation status.

- **Engagement Review**
  - Roadmap vs. committed milestones.
  - Change requests status and impact summary.
  - Architectural decisions and upcoming non-functional risks.

- **Squad Level**
  - Sprint planning artifacts, velocity and burn-down charts.
  - Definition of Ready / Done and checklists.
  - Retrospective actions tracked and followed up.

# Defect Management & Service Levels

## 1. Defect Lifecycle

1. Logged
2. Triaged (severity & priority assigned)
3. Assigned
4. Fixed
5. Verified
6. Closed or Reopened

## 2. Severity and SLA Matrix

| Severity | Example Impact                                        | Response Time | Resolution Target          |
|:-------- |:------------------------------------------------------|:------------- |:---------------------------|
| Critical | System unavailable or data corruption                 | 30 minutes    | 4 hours or workaround      |
| High     | Key business function not usable                      | 2 hours       | 1 business day             |
| Medium   | Non-critical function affected, workaround available  | 1 business day| Next scheduled sprint      |
| Low      | Cosmetic issues or minor inconvenience                | Best effort   | Backlog prioritization     |

## 3. Reporting

- Daily defect status report during critical phases (e.g. UAT, go-live).
- Weekly quality status as part of governance pack (defect trend charts, aging analysis).
